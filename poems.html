"A Cringe Apology to Her" (03/03/2024)


Why are you hurt?
Is it just me,
Or is ot the wound that's cut?


Do you even care
That you're not alone?
Is it even fair—or so one thinks—
That we're the one in affair?


I don't love you, nor do you to me;
But we kiss sometimes for our own sake.
I got nothing, but the life's stew;
"Damn, man! Shit's not a piece of cake."


I have a shot at things,
For curiosity wins me over.
'Tis sometimes I fail the ding,
And the games fuck me over.


Here's me, writing a poetry
For—I guess—the second time.
But for you, Arpita, I hope to
Let's play Sky sometime!
