To the best girl forever (06/06/2024)

Did I ever say that you're the one for me?
I have forever held this in my heart,
My sunken love for thee.
And I never knew, you had a similar part.
I wish until the end, it's just we.

You, pretty girl, are stranded in the mist;
Amidst it all, you must pave your way.
Don't lose hope, don't let go, don't think you missed.
I will be on the other side, faraway;
It's not something that's forever, miss.

What is forever, though, is our bond,
Our love, our trust, our need of each-other.
Our language don't need a single nod;
We are meant to be forever,
Like a lightning stricken lightning-rod.

Hold on to the high ground,
The pain in thy heart shall vanish away.
Hold me like you'd never dismount,
It won't take long for you to pave the way.
And hence can we scale this great mount.

Even if you think so, you're none to blame.
You did here nothing wrong;
Why do you think you ignited this flame?
That's why I want you to be strong;
And no, it's not "the same".

In the end, it'll be just us;
No matter when, no matter where.
So, please, hold on and discuss
With yourself about what you scare.
Stay strong, and we'll be together forever thus.

I Know How Much You Love Me. (09/08/2024)

O' my dear —
Forgive all what I said;
I have my fear
That I'd lose you someday.

That I'd be alone, no one's near;
That I'd be devoid, of love that we made;
That I'd speak to myself, no one'd hear.
Don't want to wake up, up to such a day.

I've said things — I don't want to repeat,
I don't want to think about.
Want to hold you close, caress, after my own defeat;
Don't want to lose, we can talk and make it out.

Things did go bad, but we withstood the heat;
I cried, shouted, it might've been too loud.
My wounds have pierced you right through that beat;
But we stand still, we are together without no one's around.

Would you, my love, please take this apology?
The fight we had, was an opportunity:
To improve ourselves, to learn us better,
To cry, to learn to adore the bad weather.

I still feel sorry for all that I've said,
But in the end, you have taken my heart.
I'll love you even after it breaks. (You wouldn't let it.)
If I would've said something, I should've paid
The prices I couldn't afford, to lose someone
That I so much adore.
