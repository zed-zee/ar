These little 24 hours shall be
your best ones in a while, may we?
Because your happiness fuels many's,
fills fat with fulfillment, even to the skinny.


And when it comes to me,
I could've never guessed what we could be
or what we could have, but now I know
that who I love is a glacier's water flow.


The purest, the kind,
the prettiest strings intertwined.
O girl, you may not reckon your worth,
I bet the best thing to me isn't but your birth.


Und wann sage ich „ich liebe dich doch“
dann meine ich alles von dir, ∞ noch.

I l♡ve you more than you think
and more than the times anyone could blink.
May count it, but not the sheeps today
because each second leads to a new play.


A new day, begin the new age
by understanding the now set-up stage
and execute not what one wants
but what you need is all that counts.


Be yourself and live up to the hype
I'll be forever loving you your type.
Be yourself but change for good
for that's a nice attitude under the hood
.

Coming back to me and us,
our first time together, no fuss,
and till the eternal lasts
shall come no last pasts.


Tick-tock on the clock,
Click-clock and watch our lips lock
and adoring each elementary time
for winning over you, calling you 'mine'.


You are the best in each aspect,
so, my girl, you own all my respect.
I will be loving you right,
till the end, holding you tight.


Vierzehn, sechzehn, siebzehn und siebzig,
zählen bis infinity für ich, mich, du, dich.
For me and us together, you shan't worry,
one day we will get married.
